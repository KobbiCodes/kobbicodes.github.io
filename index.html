    About

    I'm a computer science graduate from Spain that's recently moved to Sweden to pursue a career in game development. Currently, I'm working as a contracted, freelance game programmer.
    Interpersonally, I'm very much a people's person. I work best in multidisciplinary teams, where my expertise can bridge technical gaps and help realize the team's creative vision. I'm at my most fulfilled when my solutions are easily understood, maintained, and help others shine. (Maybe that's why I end up playing support/healer in most games I play!)
    Outside of games, my hobbies mostly involve tabletop gaming. I've been running TTRPGs for a decade and a half with many different groups in that time. I have a particular soft spot for Pathfinder 2e and LANCER, though I've played many, many others. I've also written a few D&D adventures that achieved best seller status on DMsGuild.
